What is a hardware??

Some people may may not know what a hardware is, so that's why I'm here to explain. By definition a computer hardware is the collection of physical parts of a computer system. This includes the computer case, monitor, keyboard, and mouse. It also includes all the parts inside the computer case, such as the hard disk drive, motherboard, video card, and many others. Computer hardware is what you can physically touch. So you can see that a computer needs a computer hardware, and it would be pretty hard to make a computer without a hardware.

Why is a hardware needed?

Well if you read the last paragraph a hardware is connected to everything and it pretty makes everythingwork. It is what makes the keyboards, monitors and everything esle in the computer work. The hardware is pretty much a combination of the brain and a heart of something. The reason I say this is because the hardware lets a computer turn on a computer, like a heart, and the hardware is always doing things and working, just like the brain.
